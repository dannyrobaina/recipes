Ingredients:
 - 1 lb dried chick peas cooked
 - 6 cloves garlic, peeled, and chopped
 - 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
 - 1 cup tahini (sesame seed paste)
 - salt and white pepper to taste
 - 1/4 minced fresh parsley
 
Directions:
 1. Cook beans according to packaging directions. Drain, reserve liquid.
 
 2. Place chick peas, garlic, lemon juice and tahini in a food processor fitted with metal blades.
    Blend until smooth, adding reserved liquid as you go to make a thic puree
	
 3. Season with salt and pepper.
 
Makes approx. 5 cups. Server with pita bread or crudites
